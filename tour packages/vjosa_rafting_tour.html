Overview

Rafting in Albania, in the beauty of the last wild river in Europe-The Vjosa River!

Come with us to enjoy the adrenaline of rafting in the Vjosa river.

We depart early in the morning around 7:00 AM from Tirana through a scenic road to Permet where our Rafting adventure will take part. After reaching the starting point and having a cup of coffee or a beverage we start the Adventure at 10:00 AM. After around 2 hours of rafting in the waters of Vjosa we reach the city of Permet.

We have around 2 hours free time to go around the city and discover its attractions or enjoy a nice meal before we hit the road again towards Gjirokastra.

This magnificent day ends around 4:00 PM in Cerciz Topulli square, right where it began! In the afternoon we take our vehicles to go back to the city of tirana.

Notes:

  • During the summer season comfortable, light clothes are suggested.
  • Passenger must be able to walk long distances.
